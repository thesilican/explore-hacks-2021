An A.I.-powered Discord bot that monitors messages for at-risk content and anonymously connects people who need a healthy outlet for their thoughts.

What is Wellness Bot?

INVITE TO YOUR SERVER

The Wellness Bot was created with one thing in mind: Keeping the people in your server safe. Wellness Bot uses machine learning and artificial intelligence to scan through messages sent through your server and determine whether or not the content being sent is potentially harmful to the user. This includes mentions of depression, self-harm, and suicidal ideation. Wellness Bot's main goal is intervention and stepping in to help users who may be at risk.

The Wellness Bot also includes features that allow users to anonymously connect with others, giving them a safe, confidential place to talk to another person and cope healthily.

Wellness Bot Features

Message Monitoring

Wellness Bot scans through every message sent in a server and checks for potentially harmful content.

Chat with others

Through Wellness Bot, you can anonymously speak with other users, giving you a completely confidential platform to express your thoughts.

Become a Mentor

Through Wellness Bot, you can sign up to be an anonymous mentor and help people through tough times, completely on your own terms.

Give Feedback

Through Wellness Bot, you can give us your feedback. Wellness Bot is always seeking to improve, and we want to hear from you!
